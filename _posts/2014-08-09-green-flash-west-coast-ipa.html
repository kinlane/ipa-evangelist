There are very few beers I give a five star rating in Untappd. I tend to be very liberal in rating beers with a four star, but you have to be a damn good beer to get five stars.

One consistently five star IPA is Green Flash Brewing's West Coast IPA™. This full blown, golden IPA is the shit!

The West Cost IPA is usually on tap locally at Najas or Halibut corner in Redondo Beach. So grabbing a pint or even sharing a pitcher of it regularly, while watching the sun set over the harbor is an essential.

Right now the only beer I'll grab over a West Coast IPA, is if they happen to have Pliny the Elder on tap--otherwise it is a Green Flash.
